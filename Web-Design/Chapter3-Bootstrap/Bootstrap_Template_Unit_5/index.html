Mark Zuckerberg

I built and co-founded the social networking website Facebook.

I remember having pizza with my friends a day or two after I opened up the first version of Facebook and at the time I thought, "You know, someone needs to build a service like this for the world." But I just never thought that we'd be the ones to help do it. And I think a lot of what it comes down to is we just cared more.
