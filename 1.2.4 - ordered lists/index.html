Apple Pie

This is a recipe for a delicious apple pie.

Peom

The mighty apple
Tasty encrusted in gold
Sweet slice of summer

Ingredients

  • 3-4 medium apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 stick unsalted butter
  • 2 premade pie crusts

Directions

  1. Place bottom crust in pan.
  2. Add apples.
  3. Sprinkle with sugar and nutmeg.
  4. Top with butter.
  5. Place top crust on top and poke holes.
  6. Bake at 365 degrees F for one hour.
